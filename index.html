About Me

Rendezvous | Joeremy Christie Ong

Hello! I am Rendezvous, a creative technologist from Cagayan de Oro, Philippines.

I'm a passionate web designer, aspiring to be officially a UI/UX Designer. My journey began with a fascination for how websites looked and worked, which quickly evolved into a deeper passion for crafting elegant user interfaces.

I thrive on transforming complex ideas into intuitive, engaging, and visually appealing web solutions. I believe that the best digital products seamlessly integrate aesthetics and functionality to create memorable user experiences.

When I'm not immersed in code or design tools, you might find me relaxing in Valorant, exploring the latest tech trends, or learning about cybersecurity.
